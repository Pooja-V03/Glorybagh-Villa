OUR STORY

About GloryBagh

Nestled in the heart of Udaipur — the City of Lakes — GloryBagh Villa is a private estate where Rajasthani heritage and contemporary luxury are woven together seamlessly. Born from a vision to offer discerning travellers an escape that feels like home, yet grander in every way, GloryBagh is more than a villa — it is an experience curated for those who seek the extraordinary.

With four elegantly appointed bedrooms, twin private pools, a lush garden, and a dedicated on-site cook crafting authentic local cuisine, every moment here is designed to linger in memory long after you leave. Our team is committed to ensuring that your stay is personal, private, and utterly unforgettable.
